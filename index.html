The University of Chakwal (UOC) is a public university in Chakwal, Punjab, Pakistan, established in January 2020 by the University of Chakwal Act 2019 to provide quality higher education and research opportunities in the region. It was formed by upgrading the Government Post Graduate College Chakwal and incorporating the Balkasar sub-campus of the University of Engineering and Technology, Taxila, into a new university structure. UOC has seven faculties, including Engineering, Pharmacy, Computing, Health Sciences, and Law, with a mission to produce job-oriented graduates and promote research and innovation for Pakistan's socio-economic developmen.
